Project Overview

The City West Website project is focused on the development of a website for the resto-bar City West View. It showcases various services provided by the bar, such as food and room bookings, menus, event schedules, and customer feedback options. Customers can place orders directly through the site and make room reservations, providing an interactive and seamless experience. The project uses Python and web technologies to create an effective platform for managing customer orders, room bookings, and showcasing services.
