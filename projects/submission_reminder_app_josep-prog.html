Submission Reminder App

Project Overview

The GitHub repository submission_reminder_app_josep-prog focuses on creating an app that helps users manage their deadlines and remind them of upcoming submissions. It is designed to be useful for students, professionals, or anyone who needs to keep track of important tasks and deadlines, such as assignments, project submissions, or meetings.
