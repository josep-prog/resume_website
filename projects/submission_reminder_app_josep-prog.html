Project Overview

The Submission Reminder App is designed to help users manage their deadlines and receive reminders for upcoming tasks or submissions. Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone who needs to track deadlines, this app helps you stay organized and on top of important dates such as assignments, project submissions, and meetings.
